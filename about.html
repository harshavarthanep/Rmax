Our Journey

A timeline of innovation and connectivity.

2003

Started off as a reliable Hardware & Network Services provider.

2005

Introduced the first groundbreaking broadband service in Thiruvallur, revolutionizing suburb regions.

2007

Spearheaded Thiruvallur's first high-speed wired & wireless internet services for commercial and residential users.

2010

Initiated Computer Education program to empower underprivileged candidates with computer literacy.

2014

Secured own ISP license from the Dept. of Telecommunications, Govt. of India.

2016

Transitioned to a cutting-edge Level-3 Data Center, offering secure and scalable colocation services.

2021

Introduced cloud data center services, harnessing virtualization and advanced cloud computing.

2023

Marked the 20th year completion milestone.
